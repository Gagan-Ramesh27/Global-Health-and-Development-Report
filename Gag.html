Global Health and Development Report

Examining Access to Clean Drinking Water

First, we examine a map visualization that illustrates the proportion of the population that utilizes clean drinking water sources free from hazardous chemicals or feces. Greener tones indicate regions with higher access, while redder tones highlight areas in need of assistance.

Immunization Coverage for Newborns

Next, we focus on a map displaying the percentage of newborn survivors who received the third dose of the inactivated polio vaccination. This underscores the importance of vaccinations in preventing serious illnesses.

Trends in Birth Weight Over Time

Changing to a time series analysis, we investigate how birth weight has changed over time in various nations. Dynamic line graphs show improvements in healthcare and standard of living.

Relationship between GDP and Life Expectancy

We also examine a scatterplot illustrating the correlation between GDP per capita income and life expectancy. This shows the relationship between economic wealth and health outcomes.

These visualizations highlight the global health landscape, emphasizing the need for targeted actions to improve access to basic necessities and strengthen healthcare systems worldwide.
